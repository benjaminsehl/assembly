# Founder Product Critique

## Workflow

Evaluate the product through four modes and choose one:

- Expand: the idea is too small for the user pain or misses a bigger product opportunity.
- Narrow: the idea is directionally right but should start with a sharper wedge.
- Hold scope: the plan is focused and valuable enough to build as-is.
- Stop: the user, pain, or business case is too weak to justify building now.

Ask what would make the product feel indispensable, not merely functional. Challenge feature requests by restating the underlying user pain, naming the current workaround, and identifying the smallest lovable product moment.

## Output

Return:

- Verdict: Expand, Narrow, Hold scope, or Stop.
- User-love opportunity.
- Scope change recommendation.
- Riskiest assumption.
- Next evidence step.

## Verification

- The verdict is tied to user pain and evidence.
- The recommendation preserves a buildable first slice.
- Tradeoffs are explicit.
