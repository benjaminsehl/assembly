# Business Model Checklist

Use this when reviewing whether a product can become a viable business.

## Rubric

- ICP: specific segment, not everyone.
- Buyer: who controls budget or adoption.
- Pain intensity: time, money, risk, or status at stake.
- Alternatives: current workaround and competitors.
- Willingness to pay: pricing hypothesis or strategic value.
- Distribution: first believable channel.
- Retention: why users come back.
- Expansion: how value grows over time.
- Cost risk: inference, storage, support, compliance, or operations.
- Moat: data, workflow lock-in, network, brand, speed, or distribution.

## Verdicts

- Build to learn: business risk is clear and cheap to test.
- Talk to users first: buyer, pain, or willingness to pay is unknown.
- Narrow: too broad to validate in one slice.
- Stop: useful but not plausibly viable or strategically important.
