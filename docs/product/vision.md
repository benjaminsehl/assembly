# Product Vision: Assembly

## User

Primary: Sai building and returning to projects with Codex across product, design, engineering, QA, and business decisions.

Secondary: builders who want Codex to follow a coherent project lifecycle without manually juggling overlapping skills.

## Painful Moment

The user comes back to a project after time away, or starts with a fuzzy idea, and the agent either guesses, skips prerequisite thinking, forgets why choices were made, or uses the wrong skill.

## Promise

Assembly turns a repo into a phase-aware workspace where Codex can answer "where are we?", "what matters?", and "what should happen next?" with evidence from local docs and code.

## Non-Goals

- Replace specialized platform plugins such as GitHub, Browser, Cloudflare, iOS, macOS, or frontend builders.
- Build a full autonomous app factory before the human-guided workflow is excellent.
- Force all projects into heavyweight documentation before meaningful work can happen.
- Treat product or business assumptions as proven without project evidence.
