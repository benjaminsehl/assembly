# Product Discovery Checklist

Use this when pressure-testing a raw idea before spec work.

## Core Questions

- Who is the specific user?
- What painful moment triggers the need?
- What do they do today instead?
- Why is the current workaround bad enough to change?
- What is the smallest lovable wedge?
- What would make users pull this into their workflow unprompted?
- What evidence would make us stop building?

## Output Shape

- User and painful moment.
- Current workaround.
- Narrow wedge.
- Lovable product moment.
- Main risks.
- Evidence needed.
- Recommended next step.
